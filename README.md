# Ungrounding Kinship: A Kincentric Ecological Approach to Kinship Networks



### Abstract:
This preliminary work explores the graph encoding and inherent sustainability of  Black, Indigenous, and Afro-Indigenous approaches to kinships within the natural world. We take a kincentric ecological approach to structuring kinship networks to represent familial and reciprocal ties among humans and more than human relations.
